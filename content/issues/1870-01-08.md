This is the inaugural issue of *The Woman's Journal*, launched on January 8, 1870 from offices in Boston, Massachusetts. The first issue set out the paper's founding mission: to advocate for women's suffrage and the full equality of women in American civic life. Full issue transcription and page images are available at the Internet Archive via the link above.
