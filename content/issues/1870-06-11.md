Volume 1, Issue 23 of *The Woman's Journal* turns attention to women in the workforce, documenting the labor conditions faced by women in industrial trades and domestic service. The issue argues for equal pay and the right of women to organize. Full issue transcription and page images are available at the Internet Archive via the link above.
