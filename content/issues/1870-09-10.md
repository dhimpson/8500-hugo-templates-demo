Volume 1, Issue 36 of *The Woman's Journal* grapples with the aftermath of the Fifteenth Amendment's ratification, which granted voting rights to Black men but explicitly excluded women. The issue reflects on the fractured relationships within the broader reform movement and reaffirms the paper's commitment to universal suffrage.
