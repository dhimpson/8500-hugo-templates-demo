Volume 1, Issue 48 of *The Woman's Journal* offers a retrospective on the paper's first year of publication, surveying the state of the suffrage movement at the close of 1870. The editors reflect on legislative gains and setbacks, celebrate the paper's growing readership, and look ahead to the campaigns of 1871. Full issue transcription and page images are available at the Internet Archive via the link above.
