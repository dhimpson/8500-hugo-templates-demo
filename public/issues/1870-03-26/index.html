About This Issue

Volume 1, Issue 12 of The Woman’s Journal covers ongoing suffrage campaigns and features reporting on the state of women’s education and access to higher learning. Articles in this issue address legislative efforts in several states and the connection between educational reform and women’s full citizenship.
